How did our website began?




What is our purpose, target audience, and the decision that lead us to make this project?

Purpose

At Fashion Radar, our purpose is clear: to ignite your passion for fashion by providing a rich tapestry of style ideas and inspiration. We believe that fashion is a form of self-expression and creativity, and we are here to empower you with the latest trends, expert guidance, and a vibrant community of fashion enthusiasts. Our purpose is to be your trusted fashion companion, helping you discover your unique style and unleash your inner fashionista. We hope to further nourish on this stylish journey as we explore the boundless possibilities of fashion together.

Target Audience

Our website is a place for...

>People looking for the best prices.
>People needing to find where this brand is sold.
>People who travel to different places, local or global, and shop for that brand.

Decision by Category

We chose fashion as our category because we believe that it's more than just clothing; it's a form of art, self-expression, and identity. Fashion transcends boundaries and connects people worldwide through creativity and style. We are inspired by the transformative power of fashion to boost confidence, foster individuality, and reflect cultural diversity. Our commitment to fashion as a means is rooted in the belief that it has the potential to inspire, uplift, and empower individuals from all walks of life. By focusing on fashion, we aim to harness this universal language to enrich lives and foster a sense of belonging in a world where style knows no bounds.
